Other Facilities


Sci-Fi Theme Park provide several different services as well as facilities for you to take a break with your loved ones or your friends during your trip!
Do check out the different facilities below!.

Hotel

Our resort offers an immersive experience for those who want an extended stay in our theme park. With decorations and details that pay homage to popular science fiction films, it's the perfect place for visitors to rest for a night before they venture into the park again to visit other attractions they could not cover in a day.

Restaurant

Our theme park boasts a variety of food options but the flagship sci-fi themed restaurant is a fan-favourite. It serves exotic food options and the all items on the menu are crafted from scratch by our chefs to bring diners a unique palette and new experience.

Gift Shop

No theme park is complete without a gift shop and our theme park's gift shop is stocked with memorabilia and knickknacks that sci-fi nerds will recognise - from mini replicas of fictional spaceships to snackbars that astronauts eat in space.


Bars

The theme park is targeted at young adults (we can delete this part if yall have any other target grp in mind) and our different bars are a hit among visitors. Fitting in thematically with the rest of the theme park, visitors will find bartenders curating drinks that glows in the dark and serving bar snacks that look like they are of alien-origin.

Disability Access

Our theme park is friendly to all users and this includes people with disabilities. All areas are wheelchair accessible and our friendly guides are fluent in sign language - there will be no communication barriers.

Parking

Parking is free to all visitors and booking your tickets online will ensure that you have a parking space on the day itself. Since the car park is a stone's throw away from the main entrance, it is convenient and almost hassle-free for visitors. Should you require additional help, valet services are always on hand to help.
