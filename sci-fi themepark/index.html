About

SCI-FI Themepark is located in the heart centre of magnificent London, United Kingdom. Voted THE BEST themepark to visit for consecutively 3 years in a row! SCI-FI Themepark was opened in the beginning of 2010 and hosts around 200,000 visitors every year. With over 20 rides and 20 food carts and retaurants, our themepark has always won the hearts of people.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I make a payment?

    Payment can be made by Credit card/ Debit card/ American Express for online tickets. For walk-ins, all of the mentioned and cash in local currency is accepted. Promo code can also be applied for walk-ins.

  2. What precautions should I make before going?

    Pregnant Ladies, People with Disabilities, People with fall syndrome and People with any easily aggravated conditions are advised to check with their doctors before participating in any theme park rides.

  3. Are all the rides child-friendly?

    All the rides are child-fiendly. However some rides like the flight of terror require individuals to have a minimum hight of 140cm.

  4. How can I book the whole park for an event?

    For big bookings or any corporate events or any parties, please email us at scifi@gmail.com for more details. Or call us at +44 64738889.
